WALPOLE YOUTH SOCCER

NO DOGS ALLOWED

Just a friendly reminder to all Walpole and visiting teams that dogs are NOT allowed on any WYSA facility before, during, or after a game is scheduled to take place. To all referees, coaches, parents, and players: feel empowered at all times to remind others of this policy —in the most polite manner possible, of course— and ask any spectator with a dog to remove their pet from the field while a game is in progress. We ask all spectators to remember that while a soccer game is in session, a dog can and will serve as a distraction and possible danger to players, coaches, referees, and other parents. Thank you for your cooperation in helping WYSA create a safe environment for soccer games to be played.

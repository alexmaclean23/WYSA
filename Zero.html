ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY

Walpole Youth Soccer fully supports the BAYS Zero Tolerance Policy. All individuals responsible for a team, and all spectators, shall support the referee, linesmen and players. Failure to do so will undermine the referee’s authority and has the potential of creating a hostile environment for the players, the referee, and all the other participants and spectators.

WYSA policy for an official Zero Tolerance report from BAYS is very straightforward. If WYSA receives an official report, the following is non-negotiable:
•The individual responsible is suspended for one game and expected to apologize in writing to those impacted.
•A second official report ends with dismissal of the individual for the reminder of the WYSA season.
•Any subsequent report results with the individual being banned for life from WYSA games and events.

An official report is a written report filed by the game center referee to BAYS.
•BAYS requires -WYSA president must take action within one week.
•Unofficial reports will be handled by intown/travel director and do not necessary result in suspension.

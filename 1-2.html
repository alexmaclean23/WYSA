WALPOLE YOUTH SOCCER

GRADES 1-2

The 1st and 2nd Grade program is an In-Town program consisting of skills clinics, supervised by the New England Revolution coaches and followed by games. At this age level the boys and girls programs are held separately. The Fall 2016 program will run at Bird Middle School on Saturdays, expected to begin on September 17th and ending November 12th.


  • The 1st and 2nd grade girls program will run from 12:00-1:30pm.
  • The 1st and 2nd grade boys program will run from 3:30-5:00pm.

Each week your child should bring...

  • The reversible jersey provided on week 1 of the program.
  • A pair of cleats suitable for wet or dry grass conditions.
  • A pair of shin pads that fit under his/her socks.
  • A size 3 soccer ball with his/her name written on it.
  • A full, reusable water bottle with his/her name clearly writtn on it.

Parents please park in the school parking lot, off Washington Street, and not on the road at East Street. Please check the website each week for weather/field updates before heading to Mylod or Bird Middle School.

    BAYS Travel League for 4th Grade through High School Age Teams:


  • Walpole Youth Soccer is a member of the Boston Area Youth Soccer League. BAYS currently has over 50 member towns and is recognized by the Massachusetts Youth Soccer Association (MYSA).
  • BAYS offers two, 10 week seasons, one in the fall and one in the spring. Typically, teams in u14 and younger age groups play games on Saturdays
  • Generally, the girls teams play in the morning and boys teams play in the afternoon. The fall season will often begin the first Saturday after Labor Day weekend. The Spring season will usually begin the second Saturday of April.
  • High School age teams in BAYS - u16, u18 and u19 - play only in the Spring, typically on Sunday afternoons. WYSA will form High School age teams on an as- needed basis.
  • Within BAYS, Walpole teams play against teams from other towns based on ability level. Division I teams are the most skilled, Division II teams are next most skilled, and so on -- at each age level.Divisions III and IV often have more than one team from Walpole, placed in different sections.

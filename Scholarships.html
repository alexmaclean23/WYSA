ELLIS MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIP

Each year, the Walpole Youth Soccer Association sponsors the Robert and Irene Ellis Memorial Scholarship for two graduating high school seniors (boy and girl) who participated in the youth soccer program and are continuing their education beyond high school. Each scholarship is for $500. To be eligible, the applicant must:

- Be a graduating senior (from any high school)
- Be a resident of Walpole
- Have participated in the WYSA program
- Be continuing his/her education after high school

The Deadline is Sunday May 22nd at 11:59pm. The scholarship application should be emailed to mgallivan@walpolesoccer.org by 5/22/2015 11:59pm or sent via mail to Mark Gallivan, WYSA, 11 Bridle Path - Walpole MA 02081

Recent Winners:
The 2011 Winners were Jacqueline Teteault and Cameron McDonough
The 2012 Winners were Tim Sullivan and Christina Freiberger
The 2013 Winners were Emma Comiskey and Nolan Murray
The 2014 Winners were Lauren Regan and Colin Murray
The 2015 Winners were Abby McMackin and Liam McDonough
The 2016 Winners were Erin Pitman and Belal El-Husayni

ROSEN MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIP

Each year starting in 2013, the Walpole Youth Soccer Association sponsors the Caroleen Midura Rosen Memorial Scholarship for a graduating high school female soccer player, who participated in the youth soccer program, and is continuing their education beyond high school. To be eligible, the applicant must:

- Be a graduating senior at Walpole High School
- Be a resident of Walpole
- Have participated in the WYSA program for many years
- Have participated in WHS soccer
- Be continuing her education by pursuing a Bachelor's degree
- Have demonstrated a financial need

The Deadline is Sunday May 22nd at 11:59pm. The scholarship application should be emailed to mgallivan@walpolesoccer.org by 5/22/2015 11:59pm or sent via mail to Mark Gallivan, WYSA, 11 Bridle Path - Walpole MA 02081

Recent Winners:
The 2013 Winner was Joanna Haswell
The 2014 Winner was Karalyn Kickham
The 2015 Winner was Rosie McCarthy
The 2016 Winner was Julia Sandquist
